Our Story Sejarah Kami

In the heart of Yogyakarta, centuries of metallurgical wisdom meet the exacting standards of contemporary industrial design. SR Supply was born from a desire to preserve ancestral techniques while pushing the boundaries of mechanical tolerance.

We bridge the gap between the artisan's touch and the engineer's blueprint. Every piece of hardware we produce is a testament to the belief that precision need not be devoid of soul. It is a harmonious dialogue between raw material and uncompromising craftsmanship.
